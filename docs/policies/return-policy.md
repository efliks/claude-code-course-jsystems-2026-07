# Return Policy (Consumer Returns of Unwanted Goods)

Version 1.0 — example document for the Hardware Service Decision Copilot MVP.

## 1. Return window

1.1. A customer may return a purchased product without giving any reason within **14 calendar days** from the date of purchase.
1.2. Returns requested after 14 days from the purchase date are **not accepted** under this policy.

## 2. Condition requirements

2.1. The product must show **no signs of usage**: no scratches, scuffs, dents, dirt, fingerprints on internal components, or wear on ports and buttons.
2.2. The product must be **undamaged** and fully functional.
2.3. The product should be returned complete: original packaging, cables, chargers, manuals, and included accessories. Missing original packaging alone does not block the return but must be noted.
2.4. The product must be eligible for resale as new or open-box.

## 3. Exclusions

A return is **rejected** when any of the following applies:
3.1. Visible signs of usage or wear (see 2.1).
3.2. Any damage, including cosmetic damage.
3.3. Hygiene-sealed products (e.g. in-ear headphones) with the seal broken.
3.4. Software products or devices where a bundled license/activation has been redeemed.
3.5. Personalized or custom-configured products.
3.6. Missing essential components that prevent resale (e.g. no charger for a device sold with one).

## 4. Process and refund

4.1. An approved return requires the customer to ship the product back within **14 days** of approval; shipping instructions are provided with the approval.
4.2. The refund is issued to the original payment method within **14 days** of the returned product passing inbound inspection.
4.3. If inbound inspection finds the product's condition worse than declared, the return may be re-assessed and rejected or subject to a value reduction; the customer is informed.

## 5. Evidence requirements

5.1. The customer must provide at least one clear photo showing the overall condition of the product.
5.2. If the photo does not allow assessment of the condition (blurry, partial, wrong object), the request cannot be approved until adequate evidence is provided.
